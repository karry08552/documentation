## Introduction

The Account Management section provides users with comprehensive control over their profile, subscription, and billing details within the platform.

### Key Features
* View and edit personal details such as name, email, and contact information.
* Access plan information, renewal dates, and subscription status.
* Add and manage billing details for invoice generation and payment processing.
* Robust measures ensure the security and privacy of user information.
